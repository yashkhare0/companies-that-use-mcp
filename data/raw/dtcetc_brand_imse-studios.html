About Imse Studios

Imse Studios, founded by Evelina Hedberg in summer 2019, is an artistic brand based in Stockholm. With a passion for colors and shapes, Imse Studios offers a range of unique and expressive products. From abstract paintings to beautifully crafted pottery, Imse Studios allows customers to bring a touch of artistry into their lives. Inspired by the Swedish Archipelago and Gotland, Imse Studios captures the essence of these picturesque locations in their designs. Whether you're looking to adorn your walls with vibrant paintings or add a touch of elegance to your home with handmade ceramics, Imse Studios is the perfect destination for art enthusiasts seeking one-of-a-kind pieces.

Imse Studios's Shipping Policy

Imse Studios currently offers shipping only to Sweden. They use Postnord as their delivery company for ceramics and posters. The shipping prices depend on the weight of the package and are calculated at checkout. For local pickup at their showroom, there is no additional cost. For home delivery, the prices are as follows: 45kr for packages weighing 0-0.8kg or for posters, 63kr for packages weighing 0.8-2kg, and 95kr for packages weighing 2kg and up. All orders are sent with traceable shipping and customers will receive a notification through the email address used at checkout when the package is on its way. The order is packed within 1-5 business days and the delivery takes an additional 3-5 business days. The posters are wrapped in silk paper and placed into a natural colored carbon tube, while the ceramics are packed in carbon boxes with paper filling, wrapped in paper, and sealed with paper tape. Imse Studios strives to use recycled materials as much as possible in their packaging. Unfortunately, there is no information available regarding international shipping at this time.

Imse Studios's Return Policy

Imse Studios has a return policy that allows customers to return their order within 14 days from receiving it. However, after 14 days, no refund or exchange can be offered. To initiate a return, customers must contact Imse Studios at hello@imsestudios.com before sending the products back. The items being returned must be unused and in the same condition as when they were received, and the original packaging must be used for the return. Customers are responsible for the shipping cost of returning the items and it is recommended to use a traceable service to avoid loss or damage. It is important to note that special or custom-made items cannot be returned. The processing time for returns is not specified in the information provided.
