About Ugmonk

Ugmonk is a brand founded by Jeff Sheldon in 2008, which offers an array of goal-oriented products such as journals, pens, stationery items, clothing, accessories and footwear. The products are designed with simplistic designs, and are made with eco-friendly practices. The company provides free shipping on US orders over $125 and international shipping is available.

Who Shops At Ugmonk

Ugmonk is a brand that offers physical mediums such as writing tools, rather than digital tools. It is best suited for people who prefer writing by hand instead of typing on a keyboard, and who want to structure their life in a more tangible way.

Ugmonk's Shipping Policy

Ugmonk ships domestically and internationally from their warehouse in Downingtown, PA. Domestic orders over $125 receive free shipping, and the cost of shipping is calculated at checkout for orders under that amount. It usually takes 5-10 business days for orders to arrive in the US. For international orders, the delivery time can range from 10 business days to 5 weeks, depending on the courier.
More about Ugmonk ->

Ugmonk's Return Policy

Ugmonk offers a 30-day return or refund window for new or unused products. Customers can return used products, but they will be charged a 15% restocking fee. Shipping fees associated with the return are not covered by Ugmonk. All returns must be made within 30 days of purchase and it typically takes up to 14 days for the company to process a return. Non-returnable items include digital products, gift cards, and custom items.
